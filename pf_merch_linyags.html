Linya Clothing | Bible Series

Linya Clothing is a minimalist fashion brand inspired by the Filipino word "linya," meaning line. It blends soft streetwear with intentional design, offering apparel and accessories that reflect mindfulness, calm expression, and modern simplicity. Each piece carries subtle messages and clean visuals made to resonate personally.

Inspired by nostalgic CD aesthetics, this design centers on the theme of thankfulness. With "Grace and Gratitude" as its core message, it combines retro visual cues with calming tones. It encourages mindful reflection, wrapping modern minimalism in a vintage-inspired shell.

  • Muted Monotone Color
