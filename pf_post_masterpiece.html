Agape Ica Hukou Church

20th Anniversary | You are God's Masterpiece

This event poster is for an international Filipino church based in Taiwan. This is my entry for the design competition for the 20th Church Anniversary event poster and backdrop.

The design focuses on elements of creation and living things, combined with selected colors that symbolize life, light, and earth tones, reflecting how God created humans.

Design Elements

  • Clean Sans-Serif Typography
  • Central Illustration of God & Creation
  • Radiant Light Effect
  • Soft Green Gradient Background
  • Tropical Foliage Accents
  • Scripture Highlight in Bold Text
