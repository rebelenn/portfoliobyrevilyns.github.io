Psalm 107 | Chains are broken

This artwork visually narrates my personal testimony of faith and deliverance. The figure at the center represents myself, kneeling in a moment of raw surrender yet empowered by divine light. The broken chains symbolize freedom from spiritual bondage, sin, and fear, while the glowing cross at the chest represents the transformative power of Jesus Christ in my life. The upward light signifies God’s presence, hope, and calling, breaking through the darkness that once surrounded me.

A digital illustration portraying my testimony of redemption. The kneeling figure, bathed in divine light, breaks free from chains as a glowing cross at the heart symbolizes salvation and transformation. The contrast of darkness and golden light conveys freedom, hope, and victory in Christ.

Design Elements

  • Light Source and Illumination
  • Symbolic Centerpiece — The Glowing Cross
  • Broken Chains
  • Expressive Pose and Emotion
  • Dark to Light Contrast
  • Particle and Glow Effects
